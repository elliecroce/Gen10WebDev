Restaurant Description
Welcome to Chez Ellie, a hip new spot to warm up on a cold day. Rain, snow, or sun we will always bring a smile to your face and your heart with our hearty soups and noodle bowls. We also have a monthly specials drinks and new menus for each season!

We are opening a new location! Although we love it here in Australia we have always wanted to expand abroad. Our new spot will be in the Mile End neighborhood of Montreal. Stay tuned for more details!

Our new Fall menu has recently lauched. We have included lots of warm items to keep your bellies happy and to help your bodies thaw from this cold weather! Stop in to try it this week!

Chez Ellie experiments each month with a fun cocktail or beer. October's Drink of the Month is our new SG Special Brew which incorporates all the things you love about fall: pumpkin, candy, and apples. Intrigued? Come on in and give it a taste!
